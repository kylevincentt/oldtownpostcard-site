Common questions

Things people ask before they book.

What kind of ROI can I expect?
We sell exposure, not guaranteed leads. We can't promise a specific ROI — too much depends on your offer and how you handle calls. What we can promise is reach (9,803 homes) and tracking (scan reports on your QR code) so you can measure for yourself. Strong offers — like the Lehi Bakery's "free cookie, no purchase necessary" — typically generate same-day walk-ins. Weak offers — "10% off" — typically don't.
When does the next postcard go out?
We mail when the card is full or when the deadline hits, whichever comes first. Email us at kylevcrum@gmail.com for the current mailing's exact send date.
What if I don't have a logo or an offer ready?
We'll work with you. We have a swipe file of strong offers by industry — for example, plumber offers like "$29 sewer camera inspection (regularly $79)" or HVAC offers like "$49 duct-cleaning with any AC service." If you don't have a logo, send us your business name and we'll design something simple that works.
Who actually mails these?
USPS, via the Every Door Direct Mail (EDDM) program. We bundle the cards according to USPS specs and drop them off at the post office. You can verify the program at eddm.usps.com.
Why only 16 slots?
Two reasons. First, more than 16 ads on a 9×12 card looks cluttered and reduces engagement for everyone. Second, if we sold the same category twice, neither business would get the value of being "the only [category]" the homeowner sees. Scarcity protects your investment.
Can I cancel after I sign up?
Yes, with conditions. If you cancel before we start designing your ad, full refund. If we've started design but haven't printed yet, a small cancellation fee covers our time. Once the card is printed, no refunds — we've already paid the printer and post office.
Is Old Town Postcard Co. a real business?
Yes. It's a Virginia-based small business operated by Kyle Crum out of the Manassas / Nokesville / Bristow area. You can reach Kyle directly at kylevcrum@gmail.com with any questions before you commit.
