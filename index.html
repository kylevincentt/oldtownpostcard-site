Common questions

FAQ

What if my business is not in a traditional category?
No problem. We've got slots for plumbing, dentistry, HVAC, landscaping, cleaning, construction, real estate, and more — but we're flexible. If you have a service or product that reaches the local market, email me and we'll find a fit.
How do I know the postcard reaches the right people?
USPS EDDM (Every Door Direct Mail) guarantees delivery to every single address in zip 20112. It's USPS-verified mail, not email or digital. You get a delivery confirmation report 2 weeks after the mailing date.
Can I personalize my QR code or tracking?
Yes. The QR code links wherever you want — your website, a landing page, your phone number, a booking form, or anything else. You'll get a custom short URL and real-time scan reports.
What if I want to change my ad after the proof?
You can request tweaks at the proof stage. If changes are minor (copy, phone number, color), no extra charge. Larger redesigns may have a small fee, but we'll discuss it.
Can I upgrade from Single to Double mid-mailing?
Yes. If you're happy with your Single slot and want to double your visibility for the next cycle, just let me know. You can switch to a Double anytime.
How often do the postcards mail?
The next mailing cycle is typically 2–4 weeks away. Once you reserve a slot, I'll confirm the exact mail date and send you a timeline for design approval.
Can I cancel my subscription anytime?
Yes. Monthly subscriptions can be canceled anytime with 7 days' notice before the next mailing cycle. No long-term contracts.
